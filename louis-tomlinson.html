    Biography: Louis Tomlinson is a singer and songwriter best known as a member of the boy band One Direction. He was born in 1991 in the English town of Doncaster and grew up in a working-class family.

    Tomlinson began performing in local talent competitions and school musicals before auditioning for The X Factor in 2010. He was eliminated early in the competition but was brought back as part of the group One Direction, which went on to become one of the world's biggest pop acts.

    One Direction released five albums between 2011 and 2015 before going on an extended hiatus.

    Tomlinson released his first solo single, "Back to You," in July 2017.
