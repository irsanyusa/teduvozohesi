    Historical Context

    This photograph of Henry Ford's famous 1910 Model T Ford, was for an advertisement.

    It was taken by Harry Shipler of Shipler Commercial Photographers in 1910.

    Photo Info

    Photographer: Harry Shipler
    Date taken: April 11, 1910
    Location taken: Salt Lake City, Utah, USA
    Source: Utah State History
