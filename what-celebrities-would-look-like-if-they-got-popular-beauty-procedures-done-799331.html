    If you’re satisfied with your appearance, you’re the lucky exception among those that aren’t happy with the way they look. And celebrities are in the same boat — we’ve all seen examples of good and bad transformations in Hollywood. But even among stars, there are those that don’t like to follow beauty trends and don’t change their appearance at all. And still, they’re loved by many and are very successful.

    We at Bright Side grew curious and wanted to imagine what the following celebrities would look like if they decided to have plastic surgery. It’s hard to recognize some of them.

    9. Zoe Saldana, filler injections

    10. Jennifer Aniston, filler injections

    12. Olivia Wilde, blepharoplasty

    13. Sarah Jessica Parker, botox injections

    14. Emilia Clarke, botox injections

    15. Maisie Williams, buccal fat extraction

    16. Jennifer Lawrence, buccal fat extraction

    18. Demi Moore, decreasing the lower jaw
