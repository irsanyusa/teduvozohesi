    America’s Got Talent Season 16 is starting live shows very soon, and you could be in the audience for one of them. Fans can sign up now for tickets to appear in person at the Dolby Theatre in Hollywood.

    AGT is going on a two-week hiatus during the Olympics, before returning with live shows on August 10. Shows will air on Tuesdays and Wednesdays through the month of August and into September.

    How to Get ‘America’s Got Talent’ Tickets

    The AGT Auditions Instagram account recently posted about getting tickets to be part of the live show audience. Fans can sign up through On Camera Audiences, which lays out the rules for attending these live tapings.

    Fans must be fully vaccinated and at least 12 years of age to attend a live show. Remember that in order to be considered fully vaccinated, you must wait two weeks after your second dose of the Moderna or Pfizer vaccine, or a single dose of Johnson & Johnson vaccine.

    The ticket page has more information about proper vaccination dates, according to which show you would like to attend. When you arrive to the show, you will be required to provide proof of vaccination, as well as a valid ID. As of right now, masks will be required among audience members.

    Interested in attending? Choose your preferred date and submit your information, checking off “yes” if you are fully vaccinated according to the guidelines.

    What to Expect from the ‘AGT’ Live Shows

    This season, the show is skipping the Judge Cuts round and instead moving straight from the auditions into the live shows. The six Golden Buzzer acts will automatically move on, but the rest of the acts are up in the air.

    In the final episode of the Season 16 auditions, the judges gathered at Simon Cowell’s house to deliberate on which acts should move on. They have to narrow the competition down to 36 acts, and we won’t find out who made it until the next episode airs on August 10.

    The good news is that the live shows also mark the start of fan voting, so you’ll have a chance to support your favorite acts from week to week.
