    The Voice winner Girl Named Tom has recently announced their first upcoming tour this year. Fans are definitely excited to witness the group perform all around America.

    Girl Named Tom to Embark on Brand-New Tour This Year

    Months after releasing their holiday album One More Christmas, the sibling band is once again treating fans with a brand-new tour. On Instagram, Girl Named Tom happily announced the news alongside the final dates of the show.

    “North, South, East and West – We’re painting the country this year!” the band wrote. “How many shows will we see you at?”

    Girl Named Tom On Tour 2023 is a 35-date concert that will take place in various states and cities in America. It will kick off on March 9 in Kalona, Iowa and will end up on May 20 in Defiance, Ohio. However, before they arrive at the final date, the band will stop in states including Minnesota, Tennessee, California and Oregon. Additionally, they will perform in Utah, Vermont, Connecticut, New York, New Hampshire, and several others.

    Tickets for the first day of Girl Named Tom tour is already sold out. Meanwhile, tickets for select cities are still on sale. The rest of the available tickets for the remaining dates will be available on Friday, December 18.

    They Handed Their The Voice Trophy to Their Hometown

    Earlier this week, Girl Named Tom expressed appreciation to their hometown, Pettisville, Ohio. The siblings went to their high school and gave their The Voice trophy to serve as hall decoration.

    “The Liechty siblings are forever grateful for the love Pettisville has poured into their family and their harmony trio, Girl Named Tom. What an honor to be on the Pettisville sign!” they wrote.

    By handing their trophy over, Girl Named Tom hopes that it’ll be a reminder of “what can happen when people come together.” Furthermore, they wish to remind everyone with the trophy that “small town does not mean small opportunity.”
