    Singer Angelina Jordan, who won Norway’s Got Talent in 2014 and later reached the Finals on AGT: The Champions, recently delivered some incredible covers during a live concert. Jordan performed songs by Whitney Houston and Queen.

    Got Talent‘s Angelina Jordan Covers Whitney Houston, Queen

    Angelina recently performed at Character Media’s 2022 Unforgettable Gala. During her appearance at the event, the 17-year-old singer took on a cover of “I Have Nothing” by Whitney Houston. She showed off the powerhouse voice that took her far on Got Talent.

    “It’s truly an honor to be here tonight and to celebrate this, the 20th anniversary of this Unforgettable Gala,” Angelina said before the performance. “I just want to say that I’m so proud to be a part of this beautiful Asian community.”

    During her time on stage, Angelina also delivered an incredible performance of “Bohemian Rhapsody” by Queen. It was this song that earned her the Golden Buzzer from judge Heidi Klum on AGT: The Champions.

    Angelina opted to sing just the slower parts of the song, putting a soulful spin on the classic track. The audience applauded the impressive performance.

    Where Has Angelina Been Since the Show?

    After reaching the Finals of AGT: The Champions in 2020, Angelina signed a record deal with Republic Records. She released an original single called “Million Miles” in November 2020. In 2021, Angelina followed that up with another single called “7th Heaven.”

    In addition to her original songs, Jordan has also posted cover videos on her YouTube channel. Last year, she paid tribute to Elvis Presley by singing “Suspicious Minds.” She was also seen hanging out with AGT judge Heidi Klum, sparking speculation that she might appear on All-Stars. However, that didn’t end up happening.
