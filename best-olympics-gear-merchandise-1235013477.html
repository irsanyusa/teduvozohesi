    The 2024 Summer Olympics kicked off in Paris this past weekend, and as always dozens of brands across the globe are celebrating the global sports event with limited-edition collaborations, capsules and merch — some of which debuted for the first time at the Summer Games.

    Continuing their partnership with Ralph Lauren, the official outfitter of Team USA since 2008, this year’s U.S athletes made their entrance at the opening ceremony on July 26 in matching ensembles designed by the legacy American brand, consisting of double-breasted blazers, pin-striped button-ups and lightwash denim jeans — all available to shop on Ralph Lauren’s website.

    Meanwhile, Kim Kardashians’ Skims returns this year to design the team’s undergarments and loungewear. The brand unveiled their Paris 2024 Olympics campaign last month, starring Team USA athletes such as Sunisa Lee, Gabby Thomas and Fred Kerley. The limited-edition pieces, which the athletes will wear both during, and in between, the games, are now available to shop on the Skims website.
